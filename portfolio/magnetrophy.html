Magnetrophy

Developed in: Unreal Engine 5

Status: In Progress...

Magnetrophy was created as a board game where two players face off in a battle of opposite forces, each trying to be the first to claim the central trophy. The core objective is to strategically manipulate the trophy, drawing it across a series of tiles to reach your trophy stand and securing victory. This is achieved by playing a variety of cards that can directly or indirectly move the trophy, protect your advances, or disrupt your opponent's strategies. This game was originally created as a physical board game by a group of me and three other USC students. Since creating the game in December 2023, I have been working to translate it into a video game using Unreal Engine 5.
